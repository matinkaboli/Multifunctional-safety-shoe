About APRILCLOG

APRILCLOG, the first multifunctional safety shoe, was launched 1st April 2015.

This footwear is developed according to the newest technology. This means that it is antistatic and the safety material has a toe cap of steel.

In addition, the inspiration for the grip came from Japanese fishermen and it has obtained a max CIMAC certification. All other components are 100% certified as well.

The APRILCLOG can be washed up to 90 degrees C and sterilized at 134 degrees C. The product can be washed and sterilized after which it will still appear as brand new.

From the beginning all material and the production itself have been ISO certified and APRILCLOG has passed all quality tests.

By the end of usage, the footwear will be returned to the factory for 100% recycling to minimize waste.
