Clogwasher

Description

High-quality, clean-water clog washer with drain pump, detergent and rinse-aid dispenser, double wall, rinsing at a constant temperature and pressure.

Electronic refill and break tank with extremely strong motor pump and arms. The clog-washer machine has won an award as best technological solution for environmentally safe water consumption and recycling. In fact, the same water can be reused for an entire day (up to 30-40 washes a day). The machine will register whether the water is still clean for use or whether part of the water needs replacement (by for instance 3 litres of clean water).

Clog washer with special clog basket with room for 9 clogs and insoles. At normal washing, the washing cycle will be 3-4 minutes.

2 different washing cycles

  • »Special for medical«: 300 secs, tank temperature 55°C, temperature 90°C
  • For other uses: 300 secs, tank temperature 55°C, temperature 60°C

The best device for professional activity

  • Electronic control with touch-screen display
  • Eco-clean system for clean water refill at every washing cycle
  • Thermic and acoustic double door and wall
  • New inclined and deep-drawn welded tank
  • Built-in double dispenser for detergent and antibacterial cleansing agent
  • Door-reinforcement brackets 20/10
  • Special clog basket with room for 9 clogs and insoles
  • Fully stainless steel washing and rinsing arms for low maintenance, heavy duty, all corrosion resistant
  • Extractable double pump filter + integral tank filters
